Matcha

In late 2024 I was told my heart rate was too high and I could no longer drink caffeine that was rapidly absorbed into my bloodstream (coffee). DISATER!!! Well, I needed to find an alternative and I found matcha and tried it for the first time...I hated it. It was bitter, grassy, and nasty. So I continued to live without caffeine. About 2 months later I saw a video called "Why you may not like matcha"...I watched it and discovered something, this green tea powder has many different harvests, seasons, grades, and flavors. So, I bit the nail and bought a nice matcha powder, it was FANTASTIC. So I continued this hobby and it's been very beneficial. I've been more energized, have better skin, and I learned to like grass milk!

My Top Matcha Powders of 24-25

    1. Ikuyo by Ippodo Tea

    2.Wakatake by Markyu Koyamaen

    3.Aya No Mori by Kanbayashi
